FRANCHISE OWNED COMPANY OPERATED(FOCO)

Welcome to Swirlyo! We're on the lookout for partners who share our passion for bringing delicious treats to communities far and wide. Are you ready to join us in expanding our reach to your area? We're seeking franchisees who not only grasp our brand's vision but are also strategically aligned with our growth goals. Our franchise model operates on a franchise-owned, company-operated (FOCO) basis, where we handle everything from product sourcing to marketing strategies. As a franchisee, your responsibilities are limited to managing and overseeing the finances, while we take care of the rest. With a simple investment structure involving a franchise fee and royalties, you can enjoy the benefits of being part of the Swirlyo family without the hassle of day-to-day operations. Let's partner up and spread the joy of Swirlyo together!
